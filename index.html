Grocie. | Organic Marketplace

Grocie Organic Marketplace is my personal project, where I designed and developed a responsive website that serves as an online platform connecting consumers with sellers of organic products. UnsoedFair | Job Fair for Jenderal Soedirman College Student

UnsoedFair is my personal project, an innovative web application designed specifically for job fairs targeting students and graduates of Universitas Jenderal Soedirman. This platform aims to bridge the gap between students, graduates, and potential employers, providing a seamless and efficient way for job seekers to connect with job opportunities.
